GeoPandas: turn shapefiles into answers

GeoPandas makes GIS tasks simple in Python. Hereâ€™s a tiny recipe that we use in class and projects.

  1. Read data: load shapefiles/GeoJSON into a GeoDataFrame.
  2. Fix CRS: reproject to your working CRS (e.g., EPSG:3857 or 4326).
  3. Clean columns: rename fields, drop nulls, standardize units.
  4. Spatial join: combine layers (points-in-polygons, nearest).
  5. Export: save clean results as GeoJSON or shapefile.

Tip: for large data, try dask-geopandas or convert to parquet and keep only the fields you need.
