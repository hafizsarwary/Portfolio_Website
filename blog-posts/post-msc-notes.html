MSc Geoinformatics: what I’m learning

Quick updates from my master’s at Politecnico di Milano. I’m focusing on practical skills that help build real geospatial products.

  • Computer Architecture & Operating Systems — foundations for running data pipelines.
  • Data & Information Quality — rules and checks for reliable datasets.
  • Software Engineering for Geoinformatics (Python) — clean code for spatial apps.
  • Geospatial Data Analysis — statistics and models for spatial patterns.
  • GIS & Geospatial Processing — projections, networks, raster/vector workflows.
  • Positioning & Location‑Based Services — GNSS, sensors and indoor/outdoor fusion.
  • Pollution Measurements and Subsurface Imaging — environmental monitoring basics.

I’ll keep sharing simple, hands‑on notes as the semester goes on.
