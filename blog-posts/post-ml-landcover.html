Machine learning for land cover

We turn satellite pixels into land types (water, forest, urban). The pipeline is easy to understand:

  1. Collect labelled samples for each class.
  2. Extract bands and indices (e.g., NDVI, NDWI).
  3. Train a model (Random Forest is a solid baseline).
  4. Predict on the full image and smooth with a filter.
  5. Validate with a confusion matrix.
