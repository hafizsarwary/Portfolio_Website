About Me

Hi, I’m Hafizullah Sarwary, a Geoinformatics Engineer who loves turning data and maps into meaningful insights. I’m currently studying for my Master’s degree in Geoinformatics Engineering at the Politecnico di Milano, where I explore how geospatial technologies can help us build smarter, more sustainable cities and infrastructure. Before coming to Italy, I spent over six years working with Da Afghanistan Breshna Sherkat (DABS), the national power utility of Afghanistan. There, I worked on topographic surveys, GIS mapping, and route planning for power transmission projects — learning how precise data and teamwork can make a real difference on the ground. I’m also an entrepreneur at heart. I founded Sawda.af, Afghanistan’s first e-commerce platform for daily essentials, and earlier managed Ghazni Chief Fastfood, the first standard fast-food restaurant in my hometown. These experiences taught me how to lead teams, stay adaptable, and think creatively when solving problems. Today, my focus is on using GIS, remote sensing, and data analysis to tackle challenges in energy, environment, and urban development — finding practical, data-driven ways to connect technology with everyday life.

My Expertise

GIS & Spatial Data Analysis

Advanced experience in spatial analysis, cartography, and GIS-based decision support using ArcGIS, QGIS, and Python. Skilled in integrating multi-source data for urban, environmental, and infrastructure planning.

Remote Sensing & Earth Observation

Proficient in processing and analyzing satellite imagery (Sentinel, Landsat, ASTER) for land use mapping, flood modeling, soil classification, and environmental monitoring.

Surveying & Infrastructure Mapping

Hands-on experience with topographic, route, and contour surveys for transmission line and substation projects. Strong understanding of terrain modeling, alignment design, and project implementation.

Data Management & Geospatial Visualization

Experienced in managing geospatial databases, building interactive dashboards and WebGIS applications for data visualization, monitoring, and analysis.

Geospatial App Development

Building custom WebGIS and automation tools using Python, Leaflet/Mapbox, and REST APIs to streamline spatial workflows and deliver user-friendly mapping solutions.
